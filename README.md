Honest Lies
YouTube channel about espionage, covert operations, and the gap between the official story and what actually happened.
Niche: American covert influence worldwide, 1900–2000
Format: 20–30 min documentary, voiceover only
Audience: US/UK, 55–75, educated, done with self-congratulation
Voice: Arny — a retired American who loves his country enough to want the truth

"I tell you how the American covert hand really worked across the world — through the eyes of an American who admires the boldness but doesn't swallow the postcards."
